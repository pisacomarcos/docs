## How Houra Works

Houra is organized into four integrated modules. Each module solves a specific HR need, and they all share the same data — no duplicate entry, no data silos.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your organization">
    Sign up at [houra.app/sign-up](https://houra.app/sign-up) and configure your company profile, locations, and roles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your team">
    Import or manually create employee profiles. Assign positions, locations, and access levels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set schedules and go live">
    Publish your first weekly schedule. Employees receive instant notifications on their portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track, pay, and grow">
    Monitor attendance in real time, run payroll, assign training courses, and post job openings — all from the same platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>
