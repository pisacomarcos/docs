Houra Campus is the learning management module built directly into Houra, giving your HR team and employees a single place to create courses, share internal knowledge, and recognize learning achievements. Because Campus is connected to the same platform you use for attendance, payroll, and people management, training data flows naturally into your HR reports — no extra integrations, no separate tools.

## What you can do with Campus

Campus covers the full learning lifecycle for your organization. As an HR admin, you can build structured courses with lessons and assessments, organize internal procedures in a searchable wiki, and automatically award digital certificates when employees complete their training. Employees access everything from the **Employee Portal** app — on mobile or desktop — without needing to log in to a separate system.

## How Campus connects to Houra

Campus is not a standalone tool — it is a module within the Houra ecosystem. This means:

- **Employee data is always in sync.** When you add a new employee or change their role, their learning assignments and progress records update automatically.
- **Admins work from the Admin Panel.** You create courses, manage the wiki, and review metrics in the same panel you use for the rest of HR operations.
- **Employees stay in their portal.** Your team accesses courses and wiki pages from the Employee Portal app they already use for payslips, requests, and time tracking.
- **Reports are unified.** Learning data — completions, certificates, time spent — appears alongside your other HR metrics so you can make decisions with the full picture.

## Who uses Campus

| Role | What they do in Campus |
|---|---|
| **HR Admin** | Creates and publishes courses, assigns training, manages the wiki, views reports, and exports certification data. |
| **Manager** | Monitors the progress and certifications of their direct reports. |
| **Employee** | Takes assigned courses, reads wiki pages, and earns certificates — all from the Employee Portal. |
