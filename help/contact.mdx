## How to reach the team

The primary way to contact Houra is through the contact form at [houra.app/contact](https://houra.app/contact). The form takes about a minute to complete and helps the team route your message to the right person.

You'll be asked to provide:

- **First and last name**
- **Company name**
- **Email address**
- **Phone number** *(optional)*
- **Number of employees** — helps the team understand your organization's scale
- **Country** — so the team can connect you with the right regional support

Once you submit the form, a Houra team member will follow up at the email address you provide.

<Note>
  Response times are typically within **one business day**. If your request is urgent, make that clear in your message and the team will prioritize accordingly.
</Note>

<Tip>
  For the fastest and most helpful response, include your **company name** and **current plan** (or the plan you're considering) in the message field. This gives the team the context they need to answer your question right away, without a back-and-forth round of follow-up questions.
</Tip>
