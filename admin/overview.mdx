The Houra Admin Panel is the main workspace where HR managers and administrators run day-to-day operations. From a single interface, you can build employee schedules, track attendance in real time, process payroll, maintain employee records, manage open positions, and keep your entire team connected — without switching between separate tools or spreadsheets. Every module feeds into the others, so the data you enter in one place automatically surfaces where you need it most.

## Live Dashboard

When you first open the Admin Panel, you land on the live dashboard — a real-time snapshot of what's happening across your organization right now.

The dashboard surfaces three key areas at a glance:

- **Attendance status** — See who has clocked in, who is late, and who is absent for the current day without navigating away.
- **Pending tasks** — Salary advances awaiting approval, shift-change requests, and other items that require your action are surfaced here so nothing slips through.
- **Punctuality metrics** — Track your team's on-time rate for the day, week, or custom period to spot patterns early.

<Tip>
  Bookmark the Admin Panel dashboard as your browser's start page so you always open Houra with a full situational picture of your team.
</Tip>

<Note>
  The dashboard updates in real time. You do not need to refresh the page to see the latest clock-in events or newly submitted requests.
</Note>
